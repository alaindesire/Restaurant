Special Offers

Chakchouka

This dish of Tunisian origin has everything to seduce you. Vegetarian, it is composed of a mixture of vegetables to which eggs are added. Enjoy it with a good bread and your taste buds will be delighted.

The Mafé

Called Mafé in Senegal and Tigua dégué in Mali, this dish is very popular in West Africa. It is made with white rice, peanut sauce and meat. It can be eaten with fish, chicken or simply with vegetables.

Koshari and dough

If you are vegetarian, you will be well served with this Egyptian dish exclusively made of rice, lentils and vegetables. This is an excellent dish for those who like to eat healthy.
